Ezuri Renegade Leader

Deck Description:

Ezuri deck focused on creating big mana via combo pieces and elf tribal strategies.
Multiple tutors included to cheat out large finishing creatures or other overrun style pump spells.

Estimated cost at time of writing is approximately $500.
